Portfolio

Typography Poster

For this project, our class was tasked to make a custom typeface using the letters of the alphabet. Everyone created their own set of characters. I designed the letters “U”, “Z” and the number “7.” Using the characters, the class built a variety of posters to show off the typeface, while demonstrating our design skills. The class had agreed on naming the typeface “OHKY.”

Art Nouveau - Poster

I was tasked to create a digital illustration in the style of art nouveau, combining two seasons of the year. I created a sunflower to represent the spring season and added a snowflake in the center to represent the winter season.

Contract Slime - Web Design and Development

For this project, I was tasked to design and develop a website for the gaming students, for their gaming project. Working with the gaming team, I created the website to best present their work using photos and videos provided. The website is a scrollable single-page website.

Thesis - Educational Math App

As my thesis project, I researched how to design an educational math application for kids. I covered basic mathematics skills for students in grade four and five. I also created a variety of colourful mascots; their purpose is to make the app more entertaining and fun. I worked on developing this project for two semesters in school.

Shaving Cream - Package Design

I created three different packaging designs for shaving cream using patterns and vector graphics, each design was different, the first one was a combination shaving cream designed for men and women, the second was designed for women, and the third was designed for men. The name of the product was randomly generated.

Calendar - Poster

I created a calendar for the twelve months of the year, focusing on designing within a grid, using a unique layout, with a maximum of two typefaces, a serif font, and a sansserif font. I used Futura to display the months and numbers, and ITC Galliard Std for the days of the week.
